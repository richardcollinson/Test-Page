M. INFO

The word M.INFO is an abbreviation for the Electronic email Magazine ‚ÄùMozambique Information". M.Info is an email newsletter in English which is emailed via a bulk emailing system to 43 000 readers approx. every seven days of each month with updated information. The magazine was started in 2008 as a way to inform the public on cultural events taking place, more readers joined and very shortly we were advertising new companies and services available, many readers posting items for sale or projects they wanted to advertise.

Should you wish to post an advert, please email to us minfo@intra.co.mz in JPEG or PDF file. Please make sure that it is no larger than 1mb and no longer than one A4 page. We then insert the adverts in our publishing program and convert to adobe Pdf before we email out to our readers

There is no charge to post a once off advert. We will update our website, so keep checking for more information!

Thank you for your support and enjoy the reading.
